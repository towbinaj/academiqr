AcademiQR — App Description
============================

What It Is
----------
AcademiQR is a free web platform that helps academics, researchers, and conference presenters share resources with their audience using QR codes and themed link collections.

The Problem It Solves
---------------------
When you give a talk at a conference, you want your audience to access your papers, datasets, slides, and related resources. Today, most presenters either:
- Put individual URLs on their slides (nobody types those)
- Email a list after the talk (most people never open it)
- Use a generic link-in-bio tool that wasn't designed for academic use

AcademiQR gives presenters a purpose-built solution: create a collection of links, customize it with your branding, generate a QR code, put it on your last slide, and your audience has instant access to everything.

How It Works
------------
1. Create a collection — add links to your papers, datasets, videos, websites, or any online resource
2. Customize the theme — choose colors, backgrounds, button styles, and layout to match your presentation or institution branding
3. Generate a QR code — customize the QR code with your logo, colors, and borders, then download it for your slides
4. Share at your event — your audience scans the QR code and lands on a clean, branded page with all your resources

Key Features
------------
- Link Collections: Organize papers, datasets, videos, and resources into themed collections
- QR Code Generation: Fully customizable QR codes with logo overlay, custom colors, borders, and export to PNG/JPEG/SVG
- Short URLs: Every collection gets a clean URL at academiqr.com/u/yourname/collection-slug
- Link Library: Reuse the same links across multiple collections (e.g., your CV link appears in every talk)
- Per-Collection Overrides: Customize how a shared link appears in each collection (different title/image per talk)
- Analytics: Track page views and link clicks per collection to see which resources your audience values most
- Conference Metadata: Tag collections with conference name, event, location, and date
- Tags: Organize collections and links with user-defined tags for easy filtering
- Passkey Protection: Set a simple access code to share during your presentation (audience types it to view the collection)
- Collection Templates: Quick-start templates for common presentation types
- Profile Page: Display name, photo, and social links (8 platforms) on every collection
- Data Export: Download your data as JSON, Excel (XLSX), or CSV at any time
- Dark/Light Mode: Toggle between themes
- Drag-and-Drop: Reorder links within collections and collections on your dashboard
- Collection Duplication: Clone a collection as a starting point for a new talk
- Mobile Responsive: Works on phones and tablets for both presenters and audience

Who It's For
------------
- Conference presenters sharing papers and supplementary materials
- Visiting professors sharing lecture resources with host institutions
- Researchers sharing datasets, code repositories, and publications
- Educators sharing course materials and reading lists
- Medical professionals sharing clinical guidelines and references at grand rounds
- Anyone who presents academic or professional content and wants an easy way to share links

What Makes It Different from Linktree/Similar Tools
----------------------------------------------------
- Conference/event metadata (not just a list of links)
- Per-collection customization (each talk gets its own themed page, not one page for everything)
- Link library with reuse across collections (add a paper once, use it in 10 talks)
- QR code generation built-in (no need for a separate QR tool)
- Passkey protection for controlling access during live presentations
- Analytics per collection (know which talk's resources got the most engagement)
- Academic-first design (conference name, event fields, research-oriented templates)
- Free to use

Tech Stack (for reference)
--------------------------
- Frontend: Vanilla JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5, CSS3
- Backend: Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage)
- Hosting: GoDaddy cPanel
- Build: Vite
- Live at: https://academiqr.com
